What are Affirmations, And How Do They Work?

Affirmations are powerful statements that help in overcoming your limiting beliefs, negative thoughts to help become more self-aware & in control of your thoughts. Did you know that it takes 5 positive statements to overcome just one When you first wake up in the morning before you grab your phone, cup of joe, or anything else? It is best to wake up and state your affirmations. This will help lead to a successful day & help you to take necessary action to make your statement a reality. For example, my favorite affirmation is 'All my interactions today are filled with harmony, peace & love ' I repeat this daily to myself, and each day my interactions with others are exactly what I say they are.
